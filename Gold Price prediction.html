DATA DESCRIPTION¶
  1. Date - Date in standard format
  2. Price - It is close price which can be considered as final price
  3. Open - Price at the time of market opening at that day
  4. High - Highest price during whole day
  5. Low - Lowest price during whole day
  6. Volume - Traded Volume
  7. Chg - % Change from previous price
